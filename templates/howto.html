How to use the Income Tax Calculator

Following are the steps to use the calculator:

  • 1. Enter your full name
  • 2. Enter your Age must be between (18-50) years to use the tax calculator.
  • 3. Enter your total income.
  • 4. Select your Health insurancce rate.
  • 5. The calculator will deduct 10% from your salary for the Employee provided fund, 5.5% from citizen investment trust & Govenment tax will be deducted according to the amount your income is.
  • 6. Click calculate for the result.
  • 7. And please check the summary after the calculation. We hope you are satisfied with our service. Thank you!
